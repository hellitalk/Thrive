Step 1 of 4 · Value Ranking
What matters most to you?
Other assessments treat all areas of life as equally important, but you don't. That's their first mistake. We don't make it.

A devoted parent, an ambitious entrepreneur, an activist working for change. Each would rank these eleven domains completely differently. And every one of them is right.

Before we measure anything, we need to know what matters most to you. Take a moment. Read through the domains below. Then drag them into your truth, most important at the top, least important at the bottom.

Remember, there are no right answers here. Only honest ones.
    Step 2 of 4 · Satisfaction & Direction
    How are you doing, honestly?
    Life is fluid and things change, but this is your moment to check in without comparing or performing. We have two questions for you here.

    1) Listen to your inner voice. How fulfilled do you genuinely feel in each area right now? Give each domain a number from 1 to 10.

    2) Let's zoom out a little. Compared to the past 3 months, is each area getting better, staying the same, or getting worse?

    Remember, a 4 is not a failure. It is simply a place that wants your love and attention.
    Step 3 of 4 · Readiness
    Where will you invest this season?
    We all have limited resources: time, energy, attention, money. This is not about giving up on any area of life. It is about being strategically honest about where you can meaningfully invest right now. Trying to work on everything leads to progress on nothing.
